About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Virginia, working at the intersection of AI and climate science. My research focuses on developing deep learning frameworks such as U-Nets, GANs, and diffusion models for precipitation downscaling, bias correction, and climate risk assessment.
