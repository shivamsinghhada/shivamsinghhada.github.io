About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Virginia, focusing on AI-driven climate modeling and hydroclimatic risk. My work integrates deep learning and physical principles to improve precipitation downscaling, bias correction, and climate risk quantification. I aim to bridge AI and climate science for sustainable adaptation and resilience planning.
